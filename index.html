About

I’m a passionate student journalist with over 5 years of experience covering arts & culture. I have held LGBTQ+ media, television, and theater beats. I enjoy writing think pieces, concert and show reviews, and long form cultural commentary. I enjoy using my reporting to illuminate the connections and contentions between art and the political sphere, particularly for the queer community.

Currently based in Chicago, I’m currently an MSJ student at Northwestern’s Medill School as part of the magazine specialization.
